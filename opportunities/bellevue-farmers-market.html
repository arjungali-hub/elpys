Bellevue Farmers Market

Volunteering at the Bellevue Farmers Market puts you right in the middle of the action on a Thursday afternoon, from setting up tents and tables before the crowds arrive to staffing the info booth, helping shoppers find vendors, selling market merchandise, and processing tokens for customers using food assistance benefits. The market runs May through October in a lively outdoor setting with live music and a real neighborhood-gathering feel. This is the opportunity for you if you're outgoing and enjoy being around people and energy — you'll be talking to shoppers, working alongside other volunteers, and helping a bustling event run smoothly. It's open to all ages and needs no experience. If you're more introverted or you'd rather do focused, quiet work away from crowds, this probably isn't your best match, and it's seasonal, so it's only an option during the warmer months.
