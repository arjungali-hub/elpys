Kelsey Creek Farm

Kelsey Creek Farm is a historic city-run farm in the middle of Bellevue, home to animals like goats, sheep, rabbits, and chickens, and a beloved local spot for families. Volunteering can involve helping care for the animals, cleaning barns, and assisting at farm events — hands-on work for anyone who loves being around animals and doesn't mind the less glamorous side of farm chores. It's a charming, green setting that feels worlds away from the surrounding suburbs. This is an easy yes if you're an animal lover looking for a meaningful way to spend time around them. There are two regular programs: After School Teen Volunteers (ages 12–18, with an adult present for 12–13) and Saturday Morning Teen Volunteers (ages 15–18). One important thing to know: the popular Summer Teen Farm Program charges a fee and functions more like a paid camp, while event volunteering (like the fall Farm Fair) is free — so if your goal is free volunteering, ask specifically about the no-cost options when you email or call to get started.
