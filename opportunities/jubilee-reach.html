Jubilee REACH Thrift Store

Jubilee REACH operates a thrift store on 148th Avenue NE in Bellevue whose proceeds fund community programs for local youth and families. Volunteering means working the floor of a busy secondhand shop — sorting incoming donations, hanging and steaming clothes, organizing shelves, and keeping merchandise stocked and presentable. If you've ever enjoyed thrifting, there's something satisfying about being on the other side of it, turning a pile of donations into a browsable, welcoming store. This is a good fit if you like retail-style environments, enjoy organizing and merchandising, and want a setting that's social but not high-pressure. The one extra step is that they ask you to schedule a brief "Meet and Greet" conversation before your first shift, so it's slightly less instant than a drop-in role, but that also means you'll start out knowing the people you're working with.
