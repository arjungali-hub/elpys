KidVantage

KidVantage (formerly Eastside Baby Corner) runs a hub in Issaquah where donated children's clothing, gear, and essentials get sorted, cleaned, and prepared for families who need them. As a volunteer, you'll sort incoming donations by size and type, check that everything is clean and safe, and help pack items that get distributed to kids across the Eastside. The environment is warehouse-like and busy in a friendly way, with a mix of teen and adult volunteers working side by side. You'll like it here if you enjoy practical, tangible work and feel motivated by the idea that a shirt you folded or a toy you checked is going straight to a child who needs it. It's flexible, so you can pick shifts that fit your week. The main thing to weigh is location — Issaquah is about twenty minutes from Bellevue, so it works best if you have a reliable way to get there. Ages 12–13 need an adult with them; 14 and up can volunteer on their own.
