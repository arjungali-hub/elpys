Hopelink Food Market

Hopelink runs food markets in Bellevue, Kirkland, and Shoreline that are set up like grocery stores, letting clients shop with dignity for the food they need. As a volunteer you'll stock shelves, sort donations, and assist shoppers as they move through the market. It's customer-facing and community-oriented, and you become a familiar, helpful presence for people relying on the service. This suits you if you want an ongoing role with real responsibility and you're comfortable interacting with the public. It's a bigger commitment than most options here — there's a background check and a required virtual orientation before you start, a minimum three-month commitment, and you need to be 16 or older — so it rewards someone looking to put down roots somewhere rather than volunteer just once. To begin, you email or call to apply, then allow about a week for the background check to clear before orientation and shifts.

How to sign up

  1. 1Email volunteerwithus@hopelink.org or call 425-869-6066 to fill out the volunteer application
  2. 2Complete a background check (can take up to a week)
  3. 3Once cleared, attend a virtual volunteer orientation (90 min, held 2–4 times per month online)
  4. 4Sign up for shifts at hopelink.volunteerhub.com
