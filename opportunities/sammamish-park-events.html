City of Sammamish — Park Volunteer Events

The City of Sammamish hosts frequent park stewardship events where you'll help restore local natural areas — removing invasive plants like purple loosestrife, putting in native plantings, building trails, and restoring wetlands and streams. Events are scheduled throughout the year at parks around Sammamish, with water, snacks, gloves, and tools all provided, and they're explicitly welcoming to students earning community service hours. The setting is outdoor and hands-on, and because most events are one-time, single-day commitments, you get a lot of flexibility to pick dates that work for you. This is a great option if you like outdoor restoration work and want something you can do occasionally without signing up for an ongoing role. It runs rain or shine, so it's less ideal if you dislike working in cold or wet weather, and summer parking at some sites is limited, so plan to carpool or get dropped off. Welcoming to ages 8 and up.
